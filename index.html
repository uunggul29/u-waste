U-Waste Goal

1. Encourage Recycling and Waste Management:

A platform for people to buy and sell waste, encouraging responsible recycling and waste management practices.

2. Local Economic Empowerment:

Empowering communities by creating additional economic opportunities through the sale of waste, especially for those who are less fortunate.

3. Environmental Awareness:

Educate and increase public awareness of the importance of the individual's role in reducing waste and supporting environmental sustainability.

ABOUT


U-Waste is an innovative solution that combines information technology with Bunga Raya Bank Sampah to raise awareness of the community about cyber waste, as well as ensuring timely and quality access for target groups.

This makes it easy for U-Waste users and Bunga Raya Bank Sampah to manage and access recycling transactions efficiently, and to turn the waste into a basic need through U-Waste in collaboration with Bunga Raya Bank Sampah.
